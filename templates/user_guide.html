User Guide

Learn how to make the most of EcoTrack+ features

Energy Monitoring

Key Features:
  • Real-time energy usage tracking
  • Appliance-wise consumption analysis
  • Cost predictions and savings recommendations
  • Historical usage patterns
How to Use:
1 Add Energy Reading: Use the form on the dashboard to input your current meter reading
2 View Analytics: Check the graphs and charts for usage patterns
3 Get Recommendations: Review AI-powered suggestions for energy savings

Carbon Tracker

Key Features:
  • Personal carbon footprint monitoring
  • Activity-based impact calculation
  • Community leaderboard
  • Eco-friendly challenges
How to Use:
1 Log Activities: Record your daily activities that impact carbon footprint
2 Track Progress: Monitor your impact reduction over time
3 Join Challenges: Participate in community eco-challenges

Food Waste Management

Key Features:
  • Food waste tracking
  • AI-powered waste analysis
  • Waste reduction statistics
  • Segregation guidelines
How to Use:
1 Log Waste: Record food items that are being discarded
2 Analyze Waste: Use AI analysis for better waste management
3 View Impact: Track your waste reduction progress

Impact Dashboard

Key Features:
  • Comprehensive environmental metrics
  • Personal eco-score
  • Achievement system
  • Community statistics
How to Use:
1 Check Score: View your current eco-score and achievements
2 Track Progress: Monitor your environmental impact metrics
3 Compare Stats: See how you compare with the community
